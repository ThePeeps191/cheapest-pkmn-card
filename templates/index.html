FAQ

Why am I not getting the card I searched for/a random card?

This website scrapes eBay (a.k.a uses a bot that types the search into eBay) and then returns the first listing when sorted by prices lowest first. If you are getting a random card, it is because that is the cheapest card eBay returned. Try narrowing down you search, like Raichu GX 29/73 Shining Legends NM instead of just something like Raichu GX or Raichu card.

Hint: you can also use regular eBay filters, example search: raichu gx -mp -damaged to filter out all Raichu GX listings with mp or damaged in the listing title.
